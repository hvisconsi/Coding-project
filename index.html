Trends in Speed Dating

John Denvir and Henry Visconsi

We study dating behavior using data from a Speed Dating experiment where we generate random matching of subjects and create random variation in the number of potential partners. Our design allows us to directly observe individual decisions rather than just final matches. Our findings will not only help determine which traits are most attractive to potential matches, but also make the speed deating process more efficient.
